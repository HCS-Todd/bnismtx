River Tips Rules

Float smarter

💧

Bring water and sunscreen.

The river feels cool. The sun still wins.

🩴

Wear secure shoes or sandals.

Flip-flops disappear. River rocks do not care.

📱

Use a dry bag.

Protect phones, keys, wallets, and small essentials.

🗑️

Pack out your trash.

Leave the river cleaner than you found it.

🚌

Plan your shuttle and pickup.

Know where the bus brings you back before you float.

🦺

Watch kids and weaker swimmers.

Use flotation when judgment says to use flotation.

Rules

Plain-English summary. Always verify before your trip.

🥤

Reusable drink containers only in river “No Zones.”

City of San Marcos - Single-Use Beverage Container Ban, Sec. 58.034.

🧊

One cooler per person, under 30 quarts, in “No Zones.”

City of San Marcos Park Rules, Sec. 58.073.

🚫

No glass containers.

City of San Marcos Park Rules, Sec. 58.034.

🚫

No Styrofoam containers.

City of San Marcos Park Rules, Sec. 58.042.

🚫

Alcohol consumption/display prohibited in city parks.

City of San Marcos Park Rules, Sec. 58.033.

🚭

No smoking or vaping in city parks.

City of San Marcos Park Rules, Sec. 34.257.

ⓘ Rules change. Use the official City of San Marcos links above as the source of truth.
